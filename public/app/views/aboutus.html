About Us

Our History

Language Academy is a school for English language and Western culture. We are based in Cambridge,Massachusetts and provide unique English classes to a variety of non-native English speakers in the Boston area. We also hold fun events and parties for our students and friends to give everyone an opportunity to meet new people who want to learn English too! Our programs and classes use activities to help students improve their colloquial English and communication skills, as well as their understanding of Western culture and daily life.
