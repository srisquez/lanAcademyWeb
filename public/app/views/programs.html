Programs

Conversation Practice (CPP)

A truly unique conversation practice class that offers one-on-one speaking practice for English language learners. Our Conversation Practice Class allows you to connect with a native English speaker through culture and language in an interactive setting.

Test Preparation (TPP)

This private test preparation class is designed to allow students to study intensively for the TOEFL, TOEIC, IELTS or SATs. With flexible scheduling options and highly experienced teachers, you can prepare for any test with confidence that you will pass with flying colors.

Specialized Program (SP)

These lessons offer a dynamic environment that allows you to design an individual learning plan for your unique needs. With a private instructor you can focus on specific areas of English such as grammar, vocabulary, writing, speaking or listening.
